About GadgetHub

GadgetHub is an e-commerce platform designed to provide users with a seamless experience when browsing and purchasing modern gadgets. From audio devices to cameras and accessories, the website allows users to explore products, add items to cart, and proceed to checkout smoothly.

This website was developed as a Capstone Project for the Web Development course by Group 39. The project demonstrates our understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in building a functional and visually appealing e-commerce interface.
